Welcome to the DermVision AI framework. This platform is designed to assist in dermatological screening by combining advanced digital image processing with deep transfer learning.

Methodology Overview

  • Preprocessing: Artifacts (hairs) are segmented using mathematical morphology (Blackhat kernel) and inpainted. Contrast is dynamically balanced via CLAHE in LAB space.
  • Classification: Features are extracted using a MobileNetV2 architecture fine-tuned via Transfer Learning.
  • Attention Mechanism: A Squeeze-and-Excitation (SE) block adaptively reweights channel feature maps to focus on the lesion borders.
  • Decision Support: Provides clinically aligned precautions, skincare guidelines, and dermatologist referral triggers.
